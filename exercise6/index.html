About me Hi there, I am Jaka, a geographer and an ICT student. I hold a Master's degree in spatial planning and regional development.
I am always looking for ways improve my skills and since I was always interested in IT, I decided to apply to the ICT programme at JAMK and make a career change.
I am originally from Croatia, but now I am based in Jyväskylä, Finland. In 2020, I have decided to permanently move to Finland together with my Finnish fiancé.
Goals Having worked in GIS industry and management consulting, I developed an passion for data analysis and GIS technologies and its application to promote sustainable development.
My goal is to develop skills in software engineering, but also analytics and I hope to use these skills to work on sustainability projects. This place was made to share my professional development, work experiences and projects with potential employers, co-workers or partners.
